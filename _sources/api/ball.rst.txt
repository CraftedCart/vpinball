Ball
====

.. contents:: :local:

Description
-----------

A ball on the table. Balls can be created with ``Kicker.CreateBall`` or ``Plunger.CreateBall``, and destroyed with
``Kicker.DestroyBall``, ``Ball.DestroyBall``, or ``Trigger.DestroyBall``.
